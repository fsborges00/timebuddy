Notes and disclaimer

      
    
        
  • Time Buddy is for planning support only. Always confirm final meeting times with your calendar or organizer.
    • 
        
  • Offsets and abbreviations can shift with daylight saving changes and local government updates. Verify near transition dates.
    • 
        
  • Business Hours Overlap assumes Monday-Friday, full-hour blocks, and a local 7AM-7PM window for each selected location.
    • 
        
  • Public holidays, company-specific schedules, and half-day windows are not included in overlap calculations.
    • 
        
  • Location search may use external geocoding and can return nearby matches. Double-check the selected IANA timezone before sharing.
